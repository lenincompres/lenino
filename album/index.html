the ancient newspaper,

yet I’m bumping daily

into unfolding joy.

Cuz you

make me happy.

When I hear your name,

something you will say with that silly face

making smiles creep

over me

even when I’m

odd.

Any owner of a dog knows

happiness can possess a body

by opening a door.

And a sad day indoors,

what may seem like a chore,

is always what you need,

a walk with your bud,

smelling the roses,

strolling in glee.

Any owner of a house knows

when you pay it, you're off the ground

feeling safe and sound evermore.

And after being away,

there’s no other place

that would make you feel whole,

than arriving home

dropping your weight

where it belongs.

I know all these things

paint the picture (perfect),

though I've always rented,

and I have no dog.

make me happy.

make me happy.
